# Complete Setup Guide

This guide walks you through the complete setup process for joining NileLink.

## Step 1: Account Registration

1. Visit [nilelink.app](https://nilelink.app)
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
3. Choose your account type:
   - **Restaurant Owner**: For managing restaurants
   - **Delivery Partner**: For delivery services
   - **Business**: For enterprise solutions

4. Fill in your details:
   - Business name
   - Contact information
   - Location details
   - Business license (if applicable)

## Step 2: Verification Process

### Document Requirements
- **Business License**: Valid commercial license
- **Tax ID**: Business registration number
- **Bank Details**: For payment processing
- **Identity Verification**: Government-issued ID
- **Address Proof**: Utility bill or lease agreement

### Verification Timeline
- Initial review: 24-48 hours
- Document verification: 2-3 business days
- Bank verification: 3-5 business days
- Final approval: Within 7 business days

## Step 3: Profile Setup

### Restaurant Profile
- Upload high-quality restaurant photos
- Write detailed descriptions
- Set operating hours
- Configure delivery radius
- Add contact information

### Menu Setup
- Create menu categories
- Add menu items with descriptions
- Set pricing and availability
- Upload food images
- Configure preparation times

## Step 4: Payment Integration

### Stripe Setup
1. Go to Settings > Payments
2. Click "Connect with Stripe"
3. Follow Stripe's onboarding process
4. Verify your bank account
5. Set up payout schedule

### Commission Structure
- Platform fee: 15% per order
- Payment processing: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- Monthly subscription: $99 (optional premium features)

## Step 5: Testing Your Setup

### Order Simulation
1. Place a test order through the customer app
2. Verify order appears in your dashboard
3. Accept the order
4. Mark as ready for pickup
5. Complete the order

### Payment Testing
- Use Stripe test cards for payment verification
- Confirm payouts appear in your account
- Test refund processes

## Step 6: Go Live

### Pre-Launch Checklist
- [ ] Profile completed and approved
- [ ] Menu fully configured
- [ ] Payment integration tested
- [ ] Delivery zones set up
- [ ] Operating hours configured
- [ ] Customer support contact added

### Launch Process
1. Toggle "Go Live" in your dashboard
2. Your restaurant becomes visible to customers
3. Start receiving orders immediately
4. Monitor performance metrics

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Orders not appearing:**
- Check internet connection
- Verify account status
- Contact support if issues persist

**Payment failures:**
- Verify Stripe account status
- Check bank account details
- Contact Stripe support

**Verification delays:**
- Ensure all documents are clear
- Check email for updates
- Call support for urgent issues
